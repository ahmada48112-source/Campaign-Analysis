# Campaign-Analysis
Professional Power BI Dashboard that analyzes customer purchasing patterns, campaign preformance, product contribution, and geographic sales trend across different customer tiers and education levels.

# Overview🎗️
This project presents an interactive CAMPAIGN ANALYSIS DASHBOARD designed for business performance monitoring and customer behaviour analysis.


It brings together campaign data, customer segmentation, product purchase distribution, and country-wise purchase trend in one visual workspace.

The dashboard is suitable for ther college project because it demonstrates data visualization, business analysis, and decision-support reporting in a clear and practical formate.

# Objective🌻
The main objective of this dashboard is to help stakeholders understand how different customer groups respond to campaigns and how purchase vary by tier, education, product category and country.

It also helps identify the strongest customer segments and the areas where marketing efforts appear to be most effective.

# Dashboard Components📈

The dashboard includes the following visuals:

* A TREEMAP showing total purchase by customer tier and education level.
* A BAR CHART comparing campaign by customer tier.
* A PIE CHART showing the share of total purchse by customer tier.
* A DONUT CHART showing total product purchase distribution across product categorise.
* A BAR CHART showing total purchase by country.
* A HORIZONTAL BAR CHART showing total customer by customer tier.

  # Key Insights⛓️
* Standard customer appear to contribute the highest customer count, making them the largest segment in the database.
* High Value customers shows strong purchase activity, indicating a smaller but highly valuable auidence.
* Customer education level sucg as graduation and PhD are prominent across the purchase distribution, suggesting educated segments are actively engaged.
* Spain stands out as the strongest country for purchase in the geographic breakdown.
* Product purchase are concentrated in a few major categories, with one category clearly leading the distribution.
* Campaign performance varies by tier, which suggests marketing effectiveness differs across customer segments.

  # Business Values📊
  This dashboard can help marketing teams identify which customer tiers respond best to campaigns and which product categories generate the most purchases.

  It also supports strategic decisions such as targeting high-value segments, improving campaign planning, and focusing on high-performance regions.

  For a college project, it demonstrates a strong understanding of business intelligence concepts and dashbord storytelling.
